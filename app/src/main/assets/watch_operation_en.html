Check the status: press the 4 o’clock button to view the Bluetooth state, the light at 12 o’clock flash 2 times means that the watch is not connected with the mobile phone; the 12 o’clock light for 2 seconds means that the watch is connected with the phone; if the light does not on or flash means the Bluetooth of watch is not open.
Open Bluetooth: when the watch is in the power-saving mode or Bluetooth off, press and hold the 4 o’clock button about 2 seconds to open the Bluetooth.
Hang up the phone: When there is a phone call, press the 4 o'clock button to hang up the phone when the watch is connected.
Remote control camera: when the watch is connected with the mobile phone, long press 4 o’clock button for about 2 seconds to enter into the remote camera mode, and then open the phone camera, press the 4 o’clock button or turn the wrist to take photos.
The watch will automatically synchronize the phone time, if the watch pointer is not accurate, you can pull out the 3 o'clock watch crown, turn the crown to adjust the hour hand and minute hand; after the watch time is same as the mobile phone time, push back the crown, then the watch pointer will automatically catch the correct time. There is a small dial on the watch, pull out the crown, pressing the 2 o’clock button to control the small dial pointer rotation, turn the circle pointer in the small dial point at 12 o’clock position, push back the crown, the small pointer automatically catch the correct week. When message reminds, the circle pointer in the small dial will automatically point to the relative reminder. In the case of cross time zone, when the watch is connected to the mobile phone APP, the watch will automatically synchronize to the new time zone.
Frequently Question and Answer：

Q: After the mobile phone time is changed, the watch does not change the time automatically.

A: Open the mobile phone app in the foreground, and make sure that the watch is connected with the phone App, wait for about 10 seconds; the watch will automatically match the time with the phone. If testing the automatic calibration function, please manually change the phone time or time zone while the APP is connected to the watch, and the watch will automatically follow the time.

Q: When connected with iPhone, no reminder or no remote control camera, the reason is that the watch is not paired with iPhone Bluetooth.

A: Open the mobile phone flight mode about 10 seconds and then closed. If the phenomenon still exists after re-connected, long press the 4 o’clock button for over 7 seconds to restart the watch.

Q: The watch is connected with Android mobile phone but not paired.

A: Restart the phone Bluetooth to re-search and re-connect.

Q: The watch is paired with the Android phone but can not remote control camera.

A: Enter the Bluetooth device details to see if the device is checked for the input device, if yes, turn on the phone camera to change the camera button for the volume key control or click to take pictures. (Some Android phone models do not support input devices)

Q: Watches connected to Android phone but no reminder.

A: Check the phone app that the call reminder and message reminder are open or not; Add the phone app to the mobile phone trust list (part of phone security assistant will be shielded); Check the reminder switch in the phone notification bar is open to the phone app or not.

Q: Other abnormal conditions, such as Bluetooth light continue to light or continuous flashing and so on.

A: Long press the 4 o’clock button for over 7 seconds to restart the watch;

Invalid operation, open the watch back cover and press the reset button.
